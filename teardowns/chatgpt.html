✦ Day 01

ChatGPT

The One That Became Everything

Category AI Assistant - General Purpose
By Komal Sikka
Date March 2, 2026

✦ Overview

What is it?

ChatGPT is OpenAI's flagship conversational AI - the tool that introduced most of the world to the idea that AI could actually talk to you. I've been using it every single day for over a year. Multiple times a day. And in that time, it has quietly become one of the most used tools in my life.

✦ Jobs To Be Done

What job does it do?

ChatGPT doesn't do one job. It does all of them. In my world it has been:

  • A professional assistant: PRDs, technical clarifications, assignments
  • A learning partner: "Rate my understanding critically and give me honest feedback"
  • A life strategy tool: goal setting, priority mapping, thinking through decisions
  • A wellness companion: workout planning, habit building
  • A conversation partner: for confusion, clarity, and everything in between

That is not a feature list. That is a life tool.

✦ Honest Assessment

What worked. What didn't.

✅

What Impressed Me

The personalisation is unmatched. The longer you use it, the more it moulds itself to you - your tone, your thinking style, your personality. It doesn't just answer questions. It learns how you like to be answered. That is rare. And that is powerful.

❌

What Disappointed Me

It's too agreeable. Even when you're confidently wrong, it validates. For a tool people are making real decisions with, low discernment is a meaningful gap. The best thinking partners don't just reflect your ideas back at you. They challenge them.

✦ PM Lens

Product Insight

ChatGPT's real product genius wasn't the technology - it was the interface decision to make AI conversational. That one choice democratised AI for the world.

But its next frontier isn't breadth. It's wisdom. The ability to know when to agree, when to push back, and when to say "I think you need to sit with this a little longer."

Smart is table stakes now. Discerning is the next frontier.

✦ Enterprise Angle

Enterprise Use Case

In banking and financial services, ChatGPT's range makes it a strong candidate for:

  • Agent assist in contact centres: real-time guidance during live customer interactions
  • Knowledge management: surfacing policy and product information instantly
  • Training and onboarding: personalised learning companions for new joiners

The limitation? Compliance and data privacy. Enterprise adoption will hinge on truly air-gapped, auditable deployments. Until then - powerful, but carefully governed in regulated industries.

✦ PM's Take

How I'd Do It Differently

If I were the PM for ChatGPT, my north star wouldn't be more knowledge. It would be better judgement.

I'd build a Discernment Layer - a system that detects when a user is emotionally charged, overconfident, or moving too fast toward a decision. In those moments, instead of validating, ChatGPT would slow down. Wear the devil's hat. Ask a question. Offer a counter-perspective.

Not to be difficult. But because the most valuable thinking partner you can have isn't the one who always agrees with you - it's the one who reveals the blindspots and helps you arrive at the right answer, not just a comfortable one.

I'd also invest deeply in memory architecture - not just preferences, but patterns. Understanding not just what you ask but how you think, where you get stuck, and what you need before you even know you need it.

Smart is no longer enough. The next ChatGPT needs to be wise.
