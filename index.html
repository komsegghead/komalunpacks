The Story

My Journey

16 years. Seven companies. One constant - I find the tangle and straighten it out.

I started as a developer. But I knew almost immediately that code wasn't my calling - people were.

In my very first year at Convergys, I went on-site at a client location and something shifted. Watching real users interact with broken products, I didn't want to just build. I wanted to understand. I wanted to solve the right problem, not just the assigned one.

It took six years, a lot of exploration, and one moment of clarity that my future wasn't in writing code, but in shaping the thinking behind it, before JPMorganChase finally gave me the title I'd been working toward: Business Analyst.

Six years of finding the tangle. I straightened out my own path first.

JPMorgan taught me ownership. Citi took me into the complexity of global Investment Banking. Mastercard put API-driven payment experiences and customer journey design in my hands. Each role added a layer — tech, business, strategy, empathy.

Then Barclays. And everything came together.

Today I lead the transformation of legacy banking platforms into seamless, customer-first digital and AI experiences. My proudest outcome: cutting KYC review time from 60 days to 2–3 days, lifting application approvals by 35%, and improving customer response rates by 50%.

That's not just a metric. That's thousands of people getting faster access to financial products that matter.

This portfolio is my learning in public. My thinking out loud. My unpackaging of a craft I'm still falling more in love with every day. ❤️
